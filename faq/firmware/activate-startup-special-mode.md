# Activate Startup Special Mode

### For Q2/Q3 Devices

1. **Android Recovery Mode:**
   * Press and hold the Power Key and Volume (+) simultaneously.
   * Continue holding until the device restarts.
2. **Fastboot Mode:**
   * Press and hold the Power Key and Volume (-) simultaneously.
   * Continue holding until the device restarts.

### For Q1 Devices

1. **Android Recovery Mode:**
   * Press and hold the Power Key and the SCAN (Keyboard) button simultaneously.
   * Continue holding until the device restarts.
2. **Fastboot Mode**:
   * Press and hold the Power Key and the SCAN (Left) button simultaneously.
   * Continue holding until the device restarts.
