# Troubleshoot fastboot reason:cmd

3he device entered fastboot mode, reason: cmd.&#x20;

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (113).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

Resolution steps:

1. Press Volume- , Recovery mode menu appears

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (114).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

2. Press Power to enter Recovery mode.

<div align="left"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (115).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure></div>

3. In Recovery mode, select Reboot system now(Volume+- to select)  and press the Power button, then terminal will reboot normal
