# Access Settings Sub Menu

### Context

Terminal owners who have created custom launcher applications may restrict direct access to the standard Settings application. However, they can still provide access to specific settings sub-menus directly through their custom launcher.

### Sub-Menu Access Examples

Here are examples for launching sub-menus for Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Sound, Display, Date, Battery Saver, and other local settings directly from a custom launcher application. [Download examples](https://ftp.wizarpos.com/advanceSDK/QuickStartActivity.zip)
